Almond Cocoa Cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup almonds (whole, sliced, chopped, etc, your preference)
  • 1 1/2 cup (186g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350F

  1. Spread almonds on a baking sheet and bake at 350F for 10 minutes.
  2. Let almonds cool before mixing in at the end.
  3. With the ingredient gathered, start by mixing softened butter. An easy way to do this is by microwaving butter in 5 second increments until softened. Usually only takes 2-3 times.
  4. Mix butter and sugars together until well mixed and smooth.
  5. Add egg and vanilla extract and mix until well combined.
  6. In a seperate bowl, combine all dry ingredients. I advise to sift the cocoa powder as it can clump easily and makes mixing harder.
  7. Slowly integrate dry ingredients with wet ingredients.
  8. Add almonds and mix until combined.
  9. Optional* - Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours to overnight.
  10. Scoop cookies into 2 tablespoon servings and place on tray at least 2 inches apart from each other
  11. Flatten them a little bit as they normally don't spread much.
  12. Bake at 350F for 15 minutes, rotating tray halfway through.
