Mexican Wedding Cookie!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons water
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped almonds
  • 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powder sugar

Directions:

  1. Cream room temperature butter with sugar
  2. Add in water and vanilla extract
  3. Mix in flour and almonds until smooth
  4. Cover and chill in refridgerator for 1-3 hours
  5. Scoop into balls or desired shape
  6. Bake at 325F for 16-20 minutes or until sides are golden brown
  7. Remove from oven and let cool completely
  8. Dip cookie into powdered sugar and repeat until all are coated
