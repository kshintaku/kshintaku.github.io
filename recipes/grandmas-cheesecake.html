Grandma's Two Layer Cheesecake

Got this recipe from my grandmother, I've made my own modifications to make it into a cupcake size as well.

Serving size is one pie. Can fill 8-9" springform pan.

Ingredients:

    Crust

  • 1 sleeve graham crackers
  • 3 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 teaspoon unsalted butter
  • 3 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

    • Filling

  • 2 8oz packages of cream cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Topping

  • 1/2 pint sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoon granulated white sugar

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350F

  1. Crush graham crackers and mix with sugar and melted butter. Add cinnamon if you want as well.
  2. Spread graham crackers into pie tin and bake at 350F for 10 minutes or until slightly brown.
  3. Mix cream cheese until smooth.
  4. Add in eggs, sugar and vanilla extract.
  5. Mix until well incorporated and then pour into pie shell.
  6. Bake at 350F for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Remove and let cool while preparing topping.
  8. Mix together topping ingredients; sour cream, sugar, and vanilla extract.
  9. Pour on to of cooled cheesecake.
  10. Bake at 425F for 8 minutes.
  11. Let cool at room temperature before chilling. Best served cold.
